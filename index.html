About Me

I’m a New Englander born and bred, tossed from the suburbs of Boston to the mountains of Colorado, and lately to the food truck-laden streets of Houston.

I keep my code neat, my UI interesting yet intuitive, and try to give the user the kind of experience I would want in their position. My background in the arts has segued nicely with my career in coding. I’ve found that having an excellent visual vocabulary is an indispensible asset as a web designer. I plan impeccably to produce timely and error-free results for my clients.

Why Web Development?

I’ve always had an interest in technology, and my logical mindset has led to an easy understanding of the syntax of various web languages. My design sensibilities are useful in planning out pages, however I am equally comfortable and skilled working solely with code.
